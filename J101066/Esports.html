Consisting of 12 teams of some of the greatest players in Call of Duty, a yearly league takes place to crown one team as the champion,
this league is called the Call of Duty League.


3 game modes are used:
Hardpoint -
First team to 250 points/
the team with the most points before time runs out wins.

Search & Destroy -
Detonate/defuse the bomb
or eliminate all enemies.

Control -
Capture/ defend the two marked zones within the time
or eliminate all respawns of the enemy team
